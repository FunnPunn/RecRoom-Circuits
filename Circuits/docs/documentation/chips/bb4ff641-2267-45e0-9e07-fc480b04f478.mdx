title: 'Local Player Override Player Voice Rolloff'
---

Override the voice rolloff distance of the target player as heard by the local player.

Other players' perception of the target player's voice rolloff will be unaffected.

:::caution BETA

This chip requires beta content to be enabled in the room. You can access the setting in "This Room -> Settings".

:::
:::caution ROOMS V2 ONLY

This chip is exclusive to Rooms V2. It will not show up in Rooms V1.

:::
